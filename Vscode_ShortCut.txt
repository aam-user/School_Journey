Vscode Shortcut {

	(1)	File Explorer (Files Tab) is hidden or minimized.
		Press Ctrl + Shift + E (on Windows/Linux) or Cmd + Shift + E (on macOS).

	(2)	Open Extention Tab
		Ctrl + Shift + X

	(3)  Open Command/File finder () pannel
		Ctrl + Shift + P

	(4)  Open Setting
		Ctrl + ,

	(5)	Go To Next Line (even in middle of code)
		Ctrl + Enter

	(6)	Move Lines
		Alt + DownArrow

	(7)	Duplicate Line Up or Down
		Alt + Shift + Down/Up Arrow

	(8)	Write in More line at ones
		Alt + Ctrl + Down/Up Arrow
}
